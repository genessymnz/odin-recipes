Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pasta

Steps:

  1. Cook noodles at a boiling temperature in water
  2. Do the sacred lasagna dancing ritual while you wait for your delicious noodles
  3. Put the lasagna ingredients together
  4. Bake at the temperature your heart desires in the oven
  5. Eat!
